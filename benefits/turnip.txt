Turnips are vegetables with a creamy white color and a purple top. 

People often group this popular European staple food with root vegetables, such as potato and beetroot. 

However, the turnip is actually a cousin of broccoli, Brussels sprouts, arugula, and kale.

Medium turnip contain nutrients such as Calories, Fat, Carbs, Protein, Fiber, Sugar, Vitamins C, Calcium, Folate, Magnesium, Phosphorus, Potassium.
