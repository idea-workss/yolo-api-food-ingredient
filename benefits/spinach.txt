Spinach (Spinacia oleracea) is a leafy green vegetable that originated in Persia.

It’s considered very healthy, as it’s loaded with nutrients and antioxidants.

Eating spinach may benefit eye health because they contain zeaxanthin and lutein, which work to prevent macular degeneration and cataracts, which are major causes of blindness 

Also eating spinach can reduce oxidative stress because spinach cotains antioxidants, which fight oxidative stress and help reduce the damage it causes.

Also Spinach contains high amounts of nitrates, which have been shown to help moderate blood pressure levels and decrease your risk of heart disease
