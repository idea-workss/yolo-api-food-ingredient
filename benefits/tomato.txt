Tomatoes are the most widely used food ingredient in the world, but did you know that tomatoes are an intensely nutritious plant food ?

The benefits of consuming different types of fruit and vegetable are impressive, and tomatoes are no different. 

As the proportion of plant foods in the diet increases, the risk of developing heart disease, diabetes, and cancer decreases.

This also include tomato as tomatoes are the major dietary source of the antioxidant lycopene, which has been linked to many health benefits, including reduced risk of heart disease and cancer.

They are also a great source of vitamin C, potassium, folate, and vitamin K.
